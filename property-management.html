Why Choose Afrix Management

Own Property. Never Stress About It.

Managing a rental property in Ghana is demanding — chasing rent, dealing with difficult tenants, coordinating repairs, keeping financial records. It's practically a full-time job.

That's where we come in. Afrix Properties takes on full responsibility for your property so you can enjoy passive income without the headaches. Whether you own one apartment or a portfolio of ten units, we've got you covered.

With transparent fee structures, detailed monthly reporting, and dedicated property managers, you'll always know exactly what's happening with your investment.
