Who We Are

Ghana's Most Trusted Property Partner

Afrix Properties & Management Group was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to make real estate in Ghana accessible, transparent and rewarding for everyone — from first-time homebuyers to seasoned international investors.

Over 12 years, we've grown from a small consultancy into one of Ghana's most respected full-service real estate companies, managing hundreds of properties and serving thousands of clients across the country and in the diaspora.

Our tagline — Managing Value. Building Trust. — is the principle that guides every transaction, every relationship, and every decision we make.
