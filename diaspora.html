Your Partner Back Home

Your Eyes, Hands & Trusted Partner in Ghana

Investing in property from abroad is one of the most meaningful ways to connect with home — but it comes with real risks: fraud, title disputes, poor construction and unreliable management.

Afrix Properties was built to solve exactly this. We are your trusted boots-on-the-ground partner in Ghana — handling everything from property identification to purchase, legal verification, renovation, and ongoing management.

With over 300 satisfied diaspora clients across 15+ countries, we have the track record to make your investment safe, profitable and completely stress-free.

What We Offer

A Complete Diaspora
Investment Package

From your first property search to monthly rental income deposits — we handle every step.

Virtual Property Tours

Can't travel? We conduct live video tours of shortlisted properties so you can see every room before committing.

Full Legal Due Diligence

We verify title deeds at the Lands Commission and ensure all documentation is airtight before you pay anything.

Remote Purchase Process

Using Power of Attorney and digital signing, we complete the entire purchase on your behalf. No flights required.

International Payment Support

We support secure international wire transfers (SWIFT) with full payment receipts and financial documentation.

Post-Purchase Management

After you buy, we manage the property — tenants, rent collection, maintenance and monthly income reports to your account.

Dedicated Diaspora Advisor

You get a named relationship manager reachable via WhatsApp, email or phone throughout the entire process.
